About Us

Out On A Limb Tree Care is my family-owned and operated tree care service, serving our community with passion and expertise. As the sole certified arborist at Out On A Limb, I bring years of experience and a deep love for trees to every project I undertake. From residential properties to commercial spaces, I am dedicated to providing exceptional tree care services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

With Out On A Limb Tree Care, you can trust that your trees are receiving the highest level of care. I offer a comprehensive range of services, including tree pruning, trimming, removal, and stump grinding. Whether your trees require routine maintenance or specialized attention, I take a meticulous and safety-focused approach to ensure optimal results.

I believe in building strong relationships with my clients, taking the time to understand their goals and concerns. By providing expert advice and tailored solutions, I ensure that every tree care project is executed with precision and attention to detail.

When you choose Out On A Limb Tree Care, you can rest assured that your trees are in capable hands. I am dedicated to the health, beauty, and safety of your trees, and I take pride in delivering exceptional service that exceeds expectations. Experience the difference of a local family-owned tree care service that genuinely cares about your trees and your satisfaction.
